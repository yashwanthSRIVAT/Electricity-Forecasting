Forecasting with SARIMA and Kernel-Lasso
  • The SARIMA model will generate daily load forecasts until 2028.
  • For each month generated, the Kernel-Lasso regressor will take the last 7 days of load data and use them to predict the load for the first day of the next month.
  • To get a range of possible values, the regressor will be fed both the lower and upper bounds of the 7-day load data.
